🔒 Protected · no CAPTCHA puzzles · we never share your details

Mobile-first: big fields, camera-first upload, invisible spam protection, autosave.
What changed vs the app in the recordings. Old: enquiries arrived by email → you tapped "View Enquiry in Tradify" to leave your inbox. ETAs were typed by hand into the phone's SMS app with a guessed time and a manual signature. The customer form had tiny text, drag-and-drop upload and a reCAPTCHA. The job screen crammed six tabs across the top with a clipped button.

New: enquiries hit a rich push you can answer in one tap; a unified Inbox holds enquiries + client threads; "On my way" auto-calculates drive time and sends a branded, tracked message with no typing; the job screen uses a status stepper, thumb-friendly quick actions and clear sections; the customer form is rebuilt mobile-first. Full rationale, screen specs and architecture are in the accompanying Word brief.
